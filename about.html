Meet Our Doctor

Dr. Fora Patel — Dental Surgeon

Dr. Fora Patel is a dedicated and skilled dental surgeon with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) degree. With a passion for transforming smiles and improving oral health, Dr. Patel brings expert knowledge across multiple dental specialties.

At Perfect Smile Dental Clinic, Dr. Patel provides personalized attention to each patient, ensuring that every treatment plan is tailored to individual needs. From routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures, patients can expect compassionate care delivered with the highest standards of professionalism.

The clinic utilizes modern dental equipment and follows the latest protocols to deliver safe, effective, and comfortable treatments. Dr. Patel's areas of expertise include ceramic crowns and bridges, fixed prosthodontics, maxillofacial prosthetics, oral and maxillofacial surgery, and root canal treatments.
